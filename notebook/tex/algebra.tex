  \subsection{Fibonacci Matrix}
    Fast computation for $n$th Fibonacci $\left\{F_1,F_2,\ldots,F_n \right\}$ in $O(\log n)$:
    \[
    \begin{bmatrix}
        F_n \\
        F_{n-1}
    \end{bmatrix}
    =
    \begin{bmatrix}
        1 & 1 \\
        1 & 0
    \end{bmatrix}^n
    \times
    \begin{bmatrix}
        F_2 \\
        F_1
    \end{bmatrix}
    \]
